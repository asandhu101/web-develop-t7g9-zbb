Rides And Attractions

Major Attractions

    1. Rollercoster It is just not a normal rollercoaster that you see, you will feel the heights and the adrenaline rush through your body!
    2. Bumper Cars Favourite ride of kids who love to ride cars but not just kids it is enjoyable for adults too, enjoy the thrilling cars with their freinds and faimly
    3. Vertigo It is a marvelous and thrilling swing ride taking you almost 100ft above the ground providing you a breathtaking aerial experience
    4. Typhoon An extreme thrill ride swinging riders upside down in both directions. TYPHOON will leave you screaming for more and is sure to grab the attention of passersby.
    5. Super Shot Imagine going to extreme heights sitting and screaming with other passengers and then BOOM you are on ground in a blink, this is exactly what Super Shot offers
    6. Frenzy A pendulum-style thrill ride which takes you almost 60ft off the ground and then swings back and forth like a pendulum making the passengers experience both the upswing and downward plummet of the ride with each thrilling arc of motion
